Arches National Park

  • This site features more than 2,000 natural sandstone arches, including the famous Delicate Arch.
  • Devils Garden Campground is located eighteen miles from the park entrance and is open year-round.
  • Facilities include potable water, picnic tables, grills, and both pit-style and flush toilets.
  • Bring your own wood or charcoal for the grills.

Badlands National Park

  • The Badlands are a collection of buttes, pinnacles, spires, and grass prairies.
  • Extreme temperatures, lack of water, and the exposed rugged terrain led to this name.
  • To fully experience most of what the Badlands has to offer, it takes two days.
  • To drive through the park and stop at a few overlooks, it takes about two hours.

Carlsbad Caverns National Park

  • Carlsbad Caverns has 117 caves, the longest of which is over 120 miles (190 km) long.
  • The park is open every day, except Thanksgiving, Christmas (December 25) and New Year's Day (January 1), weather and road conditions permitting.
  • Purchase cave tickets at the ticket counter inside the visitor center.
  • Self-guided and ranger-led tours of Carlsbad Cavern are available daily.
